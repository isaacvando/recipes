# French Toast

## Ingredients

- *1* egg
- *4 tbsp* milk
- *1/2 t* cinnamon
- nutmeg
- ginger
- *1 t* vanilla
- *4 slices* bread

## Cookware

- small bowl
- frying pan

## Steps

1. Mix the egg, milk, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, and vanilla in a small bowl.

2. Dip the bread one slice at a time in the mixture until fully coated.

3. Cook in a frying pan over medium heat until tasty lookin.

4. Enjoy!
